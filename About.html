Our Name
The name “Bean Machine” comes from the origins of a statistical model. It is also known as a Galton Board and was invented by Sir Francis Galton.
It is a device for statistical experiments made to demonstrate the central limit theorem, in particular with sufficient sample size, binomial distribution, and approximations of a normal distribution curve.
